Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What are the benefits of this app?

A. Members enjoy several rewards and privileges information through this mobile app including: Exclusive offers, bonuses and savings.

Q. Do you need internet to use this app??

A. Yes the internet is required

Q. Can I use my account on another device?

A. Yes, you can, use your credentials provided you have a registered email and password to sign in

Q. How do I change password?

A. Swipe right and tap on the change password button

Q. How do I view my transactions?

A. Go to menu and tap on the transaction button

Q. How do I view my vouchers?

A. Go to menu and tap on the voucher button.

Q. How do I generate a voucher??

A. You can click on the generate voucher button on the offer listing or on the merchant listing.

Q. Can a generated voucher expire?

A. Yes, it does.

Q. How do I know the expiry date of my voucher??

A. Go to menu and tap on the voucher button

Q. What are offers?

A. These are special discounts offered for a limited period.

Q. How do I view offers?

A. Some offers are shown on the home page as soon as you login and the list of offers are seen by going to the menu and tapping on offer.

Q. How do I view merchants?

A. Go to menu and tap on Merchants and select the category that you wish to view.

Q. Do I get notifications?

A. Yes you do get notifications.

Q. How do I view my notifications?

A. Swipe right and tap on notification button or go to menu and tap on the notification button.

Q. What is statement?

A. It shows the last few credit or debit transactions performed by a member button.

Q. HHow do I logout?

A. Swipe right and tap on the log out button or go to menu and tap on log out.

Q. How do I update my personal details?

A. swipe right and tap on the profile button.
